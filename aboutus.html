Mr. Siddharth K. Ingle
Trailblazing Expertise: Guided by the trailblazing expertise of Mr. Siddharth K. Ingle, the inventor and creator of XSnap Imaging Pvt. Ltd. the company was founded with a mission to transform medical imaging and radiation technology in India.

Healthcare Transformation: Through innovation, patented designs, and dedication to excellence, Mr. Ingle has positioned XSnap Imaging as a leader in India’s medical imaging and radiation technology landscape.
